Our Story

────────── 120 Years of Brewing Fine Tea ──────────

A hundred and twenty years. It's a remarkable milestone, a testament to enduring tradition, unwavering passion, and a commitment to perfection. For six generations, our family has dedicated itself to the art of brewing fine tea, transforming simple leaves into a rich, aromatic experience that has delighted palates and brought people together for over a century. What began in a small village as a humble pursuit has grown into a legacy, passed down from one generation to the next with meticulous care and a deep reverence for the craft.

Our journey is steeped in history and an unyielding pursuit of quality. We travel to the world's most renowned tea gardens, from the misty mountains of Darjeeling to the fertile slopes of Japan, hand-selecting the finest leaves at their peak. Every step of our process, from plucking to processing, is guided by time-honored techniques. Yet, we are not afraid to innovate. We have seamlessly blended traditional methods with modern technology to ensure that every cup of tea we brew embodies a perfect harmony of flavor, aroma, and color.

The secret to our longevity lies not just in the leaves we choose, but in the people who brew them. Our master blenders are true artists, possessing an unparalleled understanding of tea's subtle complexities. They are the guardians of our family's secret recipes, meticulously blending unique concoctions that have become beloved staples in countless homes. Their dedication and skill are the heart of our operation, ensuring that the legacy of fine tea continues to flourish.

As we celebrate this 120-year anniversary, we look back with immense pride and forward with great excitement. Our mission remains the same: to share the timeless pleasure of a perfectly brewed cup of tea. We invite you to join us in raising a cup to the past, the present, and the many generations yet to come who will continue to cherish this simple, beautiful ritual. Here's to another 120 years of brewing fine tea.
